ABCs of BTC

ABCs of BTC is an online course intended to teach people about the foundations of a blockchain. Blockchain is the technology behind Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies (amongst other applications) and it's important to understand the underlying technology that makes them so secure. ABCs of BTC consists of 6 chapters and over 35 unique lessons. It will teach you what a blockchain is from a general perspective as well as the technical details and what the computers are actually doing when they "mine Bitcoin". It also covers various applications of blockchain technology including cryptocurrencies, smart contracts, and NFTs.
